Creation of Fingrams from a Knowledge Base

Generate Fingrams

This step allows to create the fingrams related to the fuzzy system previously induced from data.
Fingrams are graphical representations of the system. Fuzzy rules are represented by nodes, while edges represent the relations among rules in terms of cofiring.
GUAJE generates fingrams before and after scaling with the aim of highlighting the most relevant links.
In case you click "DO", then please wait until the fingrams window is open before going to next step.
SOFTWARE VIEW (STEP BY STEP):
1. Open the Expert View
2. Click on button "Fingrams"
3. Question: Answer "yes" to generate Fingrams
4. Set Goodness (low and high) and Pathfinder (Threshold and q) parameters and then press "Apply"
5. Select the co-firing metric and click "OK"
6. Select the graphical layout and click "OK"
7. Select a data example for the instance-based fingram and click "OK"
8. Click "START" button to begin the generation process
