Creation of Fingrams from a Knowledge Base

Visualize and Analyze Fingrams

This step allows to visualize and analyze the fingrams previously generated. Depending on the options selected, a different set of fingrams is displayed.
The first window shows the different fingrams available along with a summary of quality indicators related to the fuzzy system under design. The top part of the window allows to select the fingrams that can be viewed, while the inferior part gives information about system quality, and provides a legend aimed at making easy to understand the information embedded into fingrams.
Four fingrams are created: one complete with all the original links (Original Fingram), one pruned from the original one (PFNET: Consistency and Redundancy), one showing just the potential inconsistency among rules (PFNET: Only Consistency), and one giving a local view of the inference for the first sample in the dataset (Instance-based Fingram).
SOFTWARE VIEW (STEP BY STEP):
1. Select the desired fingrams to show in the superior panel
2. Press the button apply to show those fingrams
3. Then, the fingrams can be zoomed, moved, printed or exported
